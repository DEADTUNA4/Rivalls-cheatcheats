# 🤝 Contributing to Leafytoxins
Found a new bypass for BAC or a way to defeat service renaming? or UCG errors? We want your research! 

### How to upload your code:
1. **Fork** this repository.
2. Create a new branch for your bypass (e.g., `fix-bac-detection`).
3. Add your logic to `main.lua`.
4. Submit a **Pull Request** explaining what the code does.

### Rules for Bypasses:
* No "malware" or "loggers."
* Must be tested in **Rivals** or **Edge of Honors** first.
* Use `#` headers to explain your changes clearly.
